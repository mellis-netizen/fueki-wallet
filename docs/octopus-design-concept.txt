BEZIER CURVE EXAMPLES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Tentacle curves use SVG path syntax with quadratic (Q) or cubic (C) Bézier:

Center Tentacle:
  M 512,472        (Move to start point - bottom of body)
  Q 512,592        (Quadratic control point 1)
    512,712        (Control point 2)
  Q 512,832        (Another quadratic section)
    497,852        (End point - slight curve left)

Left Tentacle:
  M 482,482        (Start)
  Q 452,592        (Control - curves outward)
    432,692        (Middle)
  Q 412,792        (Control - continues curve)
    402,862        (End - extends left)

The organic, flowing appearance comes from:
- Multiple control points per tentacle
- Varying curve strengths
- Natural tapering (thicker at base, thinner at tips)
- Asymmetric positioning (not perfectly radial)


ICON SCALING STRATEGY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1024px:  Full detail version (all 8 tentacles, suction cups, shadows)
512px:   Full detail (minor simplification)
256px:   Reduced suction cups, simplified shadows
180px:   Good detail (use full design)
120px:   Switch to simplified version (5 tentacles, bolder strokes)
80px:    Simplified version (larger body proportion)
60px:    Highly simplified (maximum contrast, 5 thick tentacles)
40px:    Abstract (bold shapes, minimal detail)
20px:    Icon representation (simple silhouette)


EXPORT SETTINGS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SVG Source → PNG Export:
  - Color Mode: RGB (not CMYK)
  - Color Space: sRGB IEC61966-2.1
  - Bit Depth: 24-bit (8 bits per channel)
  - Alpha Channel: YES (except 1024px App Store icon)
  - Resolution: 72 DPI minimum (144 DPI better)
  - Compression: PNG optimization (lossless)
  - Anti-aliasing: Enabled (smooth edges)
  - Background: Embedded (#0F172A) or transparent

App Store Icon (1024px) ONLY:
  - Must flatten alpha channel (fully opaque)
  - Use background color #0F172A as fill
  - No transparency whatsoever


DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

"A sophisticated geometric octopus that balances organic fluidity
 with crisp professionalism - representing the intersection of
 biological intelligence and digital precision in crypto asset management."

Key Principles:
  1. Geometric yet organic (structured curves, not random)
  2. Professional not playful (institutional-grade, not cartoon)
  3. Recognizable at all sizes (strong silhouette)
  4. High contrast (accessible, visible)
  5. Brand-aligned colors (Fueki blue gradient)
  6. Meaningful symbolism (8 tentacles = multi-chain)
  7. Scalable design (SVG source, easy modifications)
  8. Platform-appropriate (iOS guidelines compliant)
